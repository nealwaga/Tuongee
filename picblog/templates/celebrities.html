Tuongee at a glance.

Tuongee is a non-profit, non-political mental health organization that aims to provide a foundation for psychological aid so as to equip generations with tools to foster mental wellness. Tubonge purposes to create a platform where mental health is addressed openly to create awareness, promote mental health and prevent development of mental health challenges.

Vision

Tuongee envisions a society where people are holistically happy, productive and immune from mental health problems.


Mission

Our mission is to provide psychological aid to prevent development of mental health challenges and create platforms where mental wellness is the foundation of a healthy generation.


Goal

To develop a mental health toolbox equipped with knowledge and strategies that will comprehensively cater for mental health needs.
